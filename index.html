ICT Association of Malawi innovation award 2022.

The Information Communication Technology Association of Malawi (ICTAM) is an industry association in Malawi that represents and advocates for the interests of the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) sector in the country. ICTAM serves as a platform for collaboration, knowledge sharing, and policy engagement to promote the growth and development of the ICT industry in Malawi.

Malawi Innovators Design Competition 2022 winner, Technology category

Malawi Innovators Design Competition (MIDC) is an annual event for innovators across Malawi to showcase their innovations, network, and compete for monetary prizes, jointly organized by Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS), Malawi University of Science and Technology (MUST) and the National Commission for Science and Technology (NCST).
